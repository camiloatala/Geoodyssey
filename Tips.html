Roatan, Honduras

This hidden gem in the heart of Central America. Roatan is a small island of the northern coast of Honduras. Surrounded by crystal clear waters, overabundance of ocean life, and one of the largest coral reefs in the world.

Egypt

Experience an enriched culture with historic landmarks around the country from the great Egyptian civilization from centuries ago. Besides the great pyramids, Egypt has great temples Luxor, Edfu, Aswan, Abu Simbel, among others.

Iceland

Iceland is divided up into 7 diferent regions with an enriched culture and beautiful landscapes. Your travel experience will vary depending on the time of year, but Iceland's natural scenery is jawdropping year round.
